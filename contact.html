Tambayoyin Da Aka Yi Sau Da Yawa

Ga amsoshin wasu tambayoyin da mutane suke yi sau da yawa

Kuna iya shigar da koka ta hanyar ziyartar shafin Ayyuka ko kuma ziyartar ofishin Hisbah mafi kusa da ku. Kuma zaku iya kiran layin gaggawa 0800-HISBAH.

Na'am, sirrinku shine muhimmancinmu. Dukkan koke ana karɓar su cikin sirri kuma ba za a bayyana sunan mai koka ba tare da izininsa.

Jami'an Hisbah ma'aikatan gwamnati ne kuma ana biyan su kamar sauran ma'aikatan gwamnati, bisa tsarin albashin gwamnatin jihar.

Idan kuna son zama ɗan Hisbah, ku tuntube mu ta hanyar ziyartar babban ofishinmu ko kiran layinmu. Ana buƙatar cika wasu sharuɗɗa na ilimi da jiki.

Dokar Shari'a ta Jihar Sokoto ta 1999 ce ta kafa Hisbah Board don aiwatar da dokokin Musulunci a jihar, musamman a fannoni na zamantakewa da shari'a.

Na'am, Hisbah yana aiki tare da hukumar 'yan sanda da sauran tsaron jama'a don tabbatar da tsaro da adalci a jihar.
