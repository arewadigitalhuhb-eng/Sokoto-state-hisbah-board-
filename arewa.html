Me ne Hukumar Hisbah ta Jihohin Arewa?

Hukumar Hisbah ta Jihohin Arewa ita ce ƙungiyar hadin gwiwa da ke tattara dukkan Hukumomin Hisbah na Jihohin Musulunci na Arewa Najeriya. An kafa ta ne domin ƙarfafa hadin kai, musayar ƙwarewa, da tsara dabarun aiki iri ɗaya a fadin yankin Arewa.

Tare da ƙarfin Jihohi 12 na Musulunci, hukumar tana aiki don tabbatar da cewa dokokin Shari'a suna aiki cikin tsari kuma al'ummar Arewa suna jin amfani daga tsaron Musulunci da adalci.

Babban ofishin hukumar yana cikin Jihar Sokoto, inda ake gudanar da taron shugabannin Hisbah na jihohi duka don tattauna batutuwa masu amfani da ƙarfafa hulɗar yanki.

Manufofin Hukumar

Waɗannan su ne manufofi masu jagorantar hadin gwiwar mu

🤝

Hadin Kai

Ƙarfafa hulɗa da hadin gwiwa tsakanin dukkan Jihohin Arewa don cimma manufa ɗaya a fadin yankin.

📋

Daidaita Dokoki

Daidaita dokokin Shari'a da ka'idojin aiki don tabbatar da tsari ɗaya a dukkan jihohi.

📚

Musayar Ilimi

Ƙarfafa musayar ilimi da ƙwarewa tsakanin jami'an Hisbah na jihohi daban-daban.

🛡️

Tsaron Yanki

Aiki tare wajen tabbatar da tsaro da zaman lafiya a fadin dukkan arewa ta hanyar hadin kai.

📊

Rahotanni na Yanki

Tattara da wallafa rahotannin Hisbah na yanki don sanin ci gaba da bukatun yanki.

🌍

Haɗin Kai na Duniya

Ƙarfafa dangantaka da ƙasashen Musulunci na waje don samun taimako da ƙwarewa.

Shirye-shirye

Shirye-shiryen Yanki

Manyan shirye-shirye da hukumar take gudanar a fadin jihohin arewa

🎓

Taron Horo na Shugabannin Hisbah

Taro na shekara-shekara inda shugabannin Hisbah na jihohi duka ke taruwa don tattauna sabbin dabarun aiki da kuma warware kalubale na yanki.

📱

Digitization na Hukumomin Hisbah

Shirin canza tsarin aiki na Hisbah zuwa na zamani ta hanyar fasahar kwamfuta da wayar hannu a dukkan jihohi.

👮

Hadakar Jami'an Tsaro na Yanki

Hadakar jami'an Hisbah da 'yan sanda na jihohi don yaki da laifuka masu yaduwa a yankin arewa.

🏥

Kiwon Lafiya da Taimakon Gaggawa

Shirin samar da taimakon lafiya da agaji ga masu bukata a fadin jihohin arewa ta hadin gwiwa.

📖

Rarraba Littattafan Addini

Haɗakar jihohi wajen buga da rarraba littattafan addini da kayan koyarwa zuwa makarantu da al'umma.

🌙

Sadarwa a Lokutan Shagali

Hadakar jihohi don tsara dabarun sintiri da tsaro a lokutan Azumi, Sallah, da shagali na Musulunci.
