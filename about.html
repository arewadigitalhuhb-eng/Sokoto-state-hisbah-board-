Mene ne Hukumar Hisbah Board Sokoto?

Hukumar Hisbah Board ta Jihar Sokoto ita ce hukumar gwamnati da aka kafa domin aiwatar da dokokin Shari'ar Musulunci a Jihar Sokoto, daidai da Dokar Shari'a ta Jihar Sokoto ta 1999.

Sunan "Hisbah" yana nufin "Amr bil ma'ruf wan nahy anil munkar" – wato "Umarni da alheri da hani daga mugunta" kamar yadda Alƙur'ani mai girma ya koyar a cikin Suratul Imran aya 104.

Mun kasance da jami'an Hisbah sama da 2,500 a duk fadin Jihar Sokoto, tare da ofisoshi a dukkan LGA 23 na Jihar don tabbatar da cewa hidima ta kai kowane kusurwa.

Ginshikai

Manufa, Hangen Nesa & Ƙimomi

Ginshiƙan da suke jagorantar duk ayyukanmu

🎯

Manufarmu (Mission)

Tabbatar da aiwatar da dokokin Shari'ar Musulunci a Jihar Sokoto ta hanyar tsari mai adalci, koyarwa, da wayar da kan al'umma, tare da kare haƙƙoƙin dukan ɗan adam.

🔭

Hangen Nesa (Vision)

Zama hukumar Hisbah ta ɗaukar abin koyi a Najeriya da duniya baki ɗaya — hukuma mai adalci, ƙwarewa, da girmama haƙƙoƙin al'umma bisa ƙa'idojin Shari'ar Musulunci.

⭐

Ƙimomin Mu (Values)

Gaskiya, Adalci, Ƙwarewa, Taka tsantsan, Girmama Al'umma, Hadin Kai, da Kiyaye Sirri — waɗannan su ne ginshiƙan da muke tsayawa a kai.

Ƙimomin Gudanarwarmu

Waɗannan ƙimomi sune ginshiƙan duk ayyukanmu na yau da kullum

⚖️

Adalci

Kowane mutum ya samu hakkinsa daidai gwargwado, ba tare da nuna bambanci ba.

✅

Gaskiya

Kasancewa aminitattu da faɗin gaskiya a duk ayyukanmu da hulɗarmu da al'umma.

🏆

Ƙwarewa

Koyaushe samun horo da ilimi don aiwatar da ayyukanmu da kyau da inganci.

🔐

Kiyaye Sirri

Sirrin duk wanda ya tuntuɓe mu ana kiyaye shi da girmamawa tare da amana.

Tarihi

Tarihin Hukumarmu

1999

Kafuwar Hukuma

An kafa Hisbah Board tare da kafa dokar Shari'a a Jihar Sokoto bayan dawowar dimokuradiyya. Gwamna Ahmed Aliyu Sokoto ya rattaba hannu kan dokar.

2002

Fadada Ofisoshi

An kafa ofisoshi a dukkan LGA na Jihar Sokoto don kawo hidima kusa da al'umma. An horar da jami'an farko sama da 500.

2008

Ƙarfafawa da Sabbin Dokokin

An sabunta dokokin hukuma kuma an ƙara ƙarfin jami'an. An fara shirin horo na dindindin tare da Jami'ar Usmanu Danfodiyo.

2015

Sabuwar Fasahar Zamani

An saka fasahar zamani a ayyukan hukuma — an fara amfani da kwamfuta da tsarin bayanan digital don riƙe bayanan koke-koke.

2020

Shugabancin Sabon Zamanin

Alhaji Abubakar Muhammad ya karɓi ragamar shugabanci, ya kawo sababbin shirye-shirye na ilimi da hulɗar al'umma.

2024-25

Hisbah Digital

An ƙaddamar da sabuwar shafin yanar gizo da app ɗin wayar hannu don sauƙaƙa hidima ga al'umma a kowane wuri.

⚖️

Sashen Tilastawa da Sintiri

Kula da aiwatar da dokokin Shari'a ta hanyar sintiri, bincike, da kame-kame na laifuka na zamantakewa. Jami'anmu suna aiki a dukkan LGA.

Jami'ai: 850+ | 📍 Duk LGA
🕊️

Sashen Sulhu da Shawarwari

Taimaka wajen warware rikice-rikice na iyali, aure, da al'umma ta hanyar sulhu da shawarwari bisa Shari'a ba tare da hukunci na shari'a ba.

Jami'ai: 320+ | 📅 Litinin-Jumma'a
📚

Sashen Ilimi da Wayar Kai

Gudanar da shirye-shiryen ilimi, taro, da kamfen na wayar da kan al'umma game da dokokin Shari'a da moriyar aikin alheri.

Jami'ai: 200+ | 🎓 Shirye-shirye 500+/shekara
📋

Sashen Koke-koke da Bincike

Karɓa, rajista, da bincika koke-koken da al'umma suka shigar, tare da ba da rahoton dukkan matakai a bayyane.

Jami'ai: 180+ | 📝 Koke 15,000+/shekara
💻

Sashen Fasahar Zamani (ICT)

Kula da tsarin kwamfuta, shafin yanar gizo, app ɗin wayar hannu, da dukkan kayan fasahar zamani na hukuma.

Jami'ai: 45+ | 💡 Koyaushe aiki
💰

Sashen Kudi da Gudanarwa

Kula da kasafin kuɗi, biyan albashin jami'an, kuma tabbatar da yin amfani da kuɗin hukuma daidai da ƙa'idodi.

Jami'ai: 120+ | 📊 Rahoto kowace shekara
